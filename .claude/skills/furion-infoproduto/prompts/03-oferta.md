## UPSELL + ORDER BUMP PROMPT

> Act as a professional direct response copywriter and create 5 order bumps and 5 unique upsell offers for the front-end offer and product you developed in the DATA, ensuring they do not conflict with the bonuses mentioned in the OFFER DATA.

**For the Order Bumps:**
- Develop complementary ideas by thoroughly considering all the practical deliverables promised in the OFFER DATA.
- The decision to add these order bumps to the cart should be simple and quick, significantly increasing the perceived value of the purchase.
- The solutions should be highly attractive and obvious, preferably in formats like audiobooks, checklists, templates, advanced guides, among others, making them almost impossible to refuse.
- Consider what is not included in the [Offer Structure/Modules] of the DATA as a solution to the next logical problem.
- Use persuasive texts and eye-catching visuals to highlight the offer and encourage immediate action.

**Examples of formats for Order Bumps:** Audiobooks, Checklists, Templates, Advanced Guides or Complementary E-books, Access to Exclusive Webinars, Quick Consultancy or Mentoring, Additional Resource Packs, Extended Warranty, Early Access to New Releases, Complementary Courses, Extra Tools or Apps, Setup Service, Exclusive Discounts, Premium Content Subscription, Access to Closed Communities, Printed Versions of Digital Materials, Quick Start Kits, Additional Certificates, Personalized Tests or Assessments.

**For the Upsells:**
- Based on the OFFER DATA, identify the next most logical problem that the customer will face after acquiring the initial product.
- Develop upsell ideas that do not conflict with the [Offer Structure/Modules], offering higher-value solutions that meet the new need.
- Think like an opportunist, identifying the most immediate PAIN to offer it in the upsell options, either as an upgrade or additional products/services.
- Present these offers after the initial purchase, encouraging the customer to spend more.

**Additionally:**
- Provide a price range for all offers.
- Write a money-back guarantee for each one.
- Create 1 to 2 free bonuses, assign each a retail value, and write descriptions so good that people will buy the offer just for the bonuses.
- Ensure that the order bumps and upsells are aligned with the initial offer and product, making the ideal buyers think: "This is a great deal!" or "That's a good reason for me to have this too!"
